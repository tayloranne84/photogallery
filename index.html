About me

My name is Taylor Thompson and I am a sophomore at Virginia Tech. I am majoring in Communication Studies and minoring in Sociology. These photos are my representation of Fall in Blacksburg, Virginia. I edited my pictures to bring out the warmth and color in them. I believe these photos accurately represent the beauty of Blacksburg. I hope you enjoy the gallery!
